WHAT IS JUNCTION?

A CREATIVE HACKATHON

Junction is a convergence point for 1.2k developers, designers, and entrepreneurs from 30+ countries. It's a place where no idea is too crazy, and everyone is welcomed.

ALL THE TECH

You have 48 hours to burn and the latest and greatest hardware and API’s at your fingertips. From drones to thermal cameras, VR Headsets to IOT components, we aim to bring it all to Junction. We don't want hardware to be the limiting factor. Need something specific? Contact us and we'll see what we can do.

OPEN FOR ALL

Whether you’re a beginner or a pro, if you have the passion and a will to create something, we want you at Junction. There's workshops, talks and tutorials suited for multiple different skill levels.

RELIVE LAST YEAR

559 Developers - Over 50K€ in prizes - 30+ Countries

Junction 2015

Organized by 100% student run Aalto Entrepreneurship Society, Junction was built to inspire and foster a community of creators and give them a hackathon experience like no other.

In 2015 Junction gathered 559 hackers from over 30 countries and let them hack on 8 different tracks and 36 different API’s for a total of 26 different prize categories and a €20,000 grand prize.
