FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

When does the application period start?

Applications open now! Apply to Junction here.

Will there be travel reimbursements available?

Yes. We will have over €50,000 in travel reimbursements.
Finland (Outside Helsinki region) 20€
Baltic & Nordics & Russia 50€
Rest of the Europe 75€
Rest of the World 150€
Interested in arranging a bus ride from your location? Get in contact with participants@hackjunction.com and get some help with it

How can I form my team?

If you don’t yet have a team we will also help with matching people up at the beginning of the event. We're also publishing a matchmaking tool to make the team building easier already before the event.

How do the tracks work? Can I only work on one track?

You can work on multiple tracks at the same time, but it’s best if you could focus on one or two.

Who can apply?

Anyone is allowed to apply! We want to allow anyone with an idea and some skills to implement it to come.

Will food be served at the event?

Yes. There will be enough food to keep everyone hacking well into the night, thanks to our amazing partners. In our registration form, please state any dietary restrictions so we can take them into consideration when making orders.

Who owns the intellectual property of hacks produced at the event?

All intellectual property produced at the event is owned solely by the participants and their team. Further plans may be discussed between the partners and the teams after the event.

What size can a team be?

Teams can be up to 5 people. If you want to hack alone, that’s totally fine. If you are still looking for a team, don't worry, we will have team building and pitching sessions at the event. You can apply at any time and join any team at any time. The team question in the application is only to make sure the entire team makes it to the event.

Does it cost to attend?

Of course not. It's completely FREE to attend!

What about accommodation?

We will have accommodation available in multiple different destinations. If you want to make sure you have a spot, book a room at our partner hotel, or hostel. More information to come.

What can I hack on?

We'll have Oculus Rifts, HTC Vives, Apple Watches, 3d printers, Microsoft Hololens, Estimote Beacons to name a few
Also check out MLH’s HW Lab. They have stuff like, Oculus Rift, Muse Headbands, Myo Gesture Control etc.

Who are the people behind this event?

We are Aalto Entrepreneurship Society, a non-profit organization completely run by students from Aalto University. We are super excited about entrepreneurship and tech events, and run multiple events throughout the year.

Are you guys arranging Pre-events and Side-events?

Yes! For example we’re having a VR-Jam in Reyjkavik. And much much more around Europe and in the Nordics. Like our Facebook page for more information coming soon :)!

How are taxes handled if I win a prize?

Prize winners shall be responsible for applicable taxes
